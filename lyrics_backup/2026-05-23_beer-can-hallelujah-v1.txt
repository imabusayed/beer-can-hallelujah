[Hook]
I pop the top and the engine dies
I see the fire in your hazel eyes
A tailgate bed and a starry sky
My beer can hallelujah, you and I

[Verse 1]
The dust is settling on this county road
We've got nowhere to be, no heavy load

[Pre-Chorus]

[Chorus]
Oh, you're my beer can hallelujah, sweet and slow
The kind of salvation that I want to know
Let the engine cool and the fire burn
With every single touch, there's a lesson to learn
